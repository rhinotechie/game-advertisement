>> How to play Space Travelers<<

I. Controls

W, A, S, D - Move
Mouse - Look
Left Click - Primary Action
Right Click - Secondary Action (Melee)
E - Use / Open / Confirm / Talk
Esc - Quit
F1 - Help

II. Objective

Complete your objective given to you at the beginning of the game.  You must gather resources from planets and alien space craft you encounter in order to stay alive.

III. Tips

1. Watch your hunger, thirst, and sanity levels.
2. Talking your way out of sticky situations is just as viable as fighting your way through them.
3. Don't die.
